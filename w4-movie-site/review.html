Review of the movie Donnie Darko(2001)

Rob Johannes, Jan, 29,2024

Donnie Darko is the type of movie that transcends genre. I have seen this movie many times and I feel I get a little different message each time. When i first viewed it I enjoyed the horror/thriller vibe it had. As I have gotten older I find myself more inclined towards the deeper meanings of the movie. I look forward to watching it again years from now and discovering what sticks out for me. This is exactly why Donnie Darko is such a powerful movie, there are multitudinous reactions you can have to this movie. Now that it is one of my favorite movies, each time I watch it something different stands out. No two viewings are the same.

Trivia about Donnie Darko

  • Jake and Maggie, real life siblings, also play siblings in the movie.
  • The movie was filmed over 28 days.
  • Frank being a rabbit was inspired by Watership Down, of which the book and movie are also featured in the film.
